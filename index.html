Wellness Tips

  • Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water throughout the day to maintain energy and support bodily functions.
  • Mindful Movement: Incorporate at least 30 minutes of activity you enjoy into your daily routine.
  • Prioritize Sleep: Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep per night to help your body rest and repair.
  • Eat Nourishing Foods: Fill your plate with a variety of colorful fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Practice Mindfulness: Take a few minutes each day for deep breathing or meditation to reduce stress.
